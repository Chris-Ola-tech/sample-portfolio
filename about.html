The Journey That Changed Everything

Everything started with a single trip to South Korea where I learnt about how teenagers had helped sponsor a conference for over 5,000 colleague youths and teens from all over the world to stay a fortnight in Soeul and deliberate on how to impact, influence and lead change in communities around the world along the lines of MDG's (and then, down the line, the SDG's).

I had always had passion and presence of mind to exceed the societal limitations of what it meant to be a teen, youth, and so on. But at this conference, the vision I had about young leaders of Change was in manifestation.

Seeds of change began with one journey, then spread.I refused to be defined; I chose to define.

My life was to take on a different approach from that conference. I wanted to be central and instrumental in raising the awareness of conscious, centered, empowered, motivated and protected youth globally and seeing this cycle repeated in communities across the globe.

This passion led to the crystallisation of my young leaders training workshops from G9 to Read Wide to Peace Corps Network to Gold Empire Foundation and then to Coach International.

In Coach International (CI) we are raising targeted students at the community levels into coaches and dispatching them to help groom peers into champions and change agents(positive models, movers¬†and¬†shakers)
