CI-MYGA WEEKEND ACADEMY

"Raising High-Quality Human Capital for the Globe"

A flagship project of The Shadrack Akrofi-Quarcoo Impact Foundation (TSAQIF)

The Vision

To bridge the gap between traditional education and the demands of the 21st-century global economy. We don't just teach subjects; we build competence, character, and community.

The Curriculum: Beyond the Classroom

Our academy focuses on the "Professional & Personal Development" missing in standard curricula. We operate through our signature S.H.E.A.T.S. pillars:

  • LEADERSHIP & THEOLOGY: Understanding moral governance and self-discipline.
  • ECONOMIC INTELLIGENCE: Financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and wealth creation.
  • SECURITY & SOCIAL SKILLS: Navigating personal safety and mastering emotional intelligence.
  • HEALTH & RESILIENCE: Fighting addictions and maintaining physical/mental peak performance.
